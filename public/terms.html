Participation

Participants attend the seminar at their own responsibility and are expected to follow the instructions of the organizers and instructors during the event.

Further practical information about the seminar schedule, venue access, and participation guidelines will be sent to registered participants before the event.

1. Registration

Registration becomes final after form submission and payment settlement.

2. Payment

Fees are payable online. Selected options modify the final amount.

3. Cancellation

Cancellation and refund rules are communicated separately by the organizer.

4. Safety

Participants are responsible for their own health status and follow instructor/organizer guidance.
