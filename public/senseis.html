Sensei Biographies

Over the coming weeks we would like to introduce you to the teachers who will attend and support this year’s summer seminar for iaido and jodo in Budapest, Hungary.

Japanese delegation

Masakazu Oshita Sensei

Name
Masakazu Oshita
Born
November 1948, Nada Ward, Kobe City
Iaido
Kyoshi 8th Dan

Oshita Sensei married in October 1974 and began studying Japanese swordsmanship at the recommendation of his wife's brother.

In 1975, he quit his self-employment and began working for the Kobe City Hall, joining the Hyogo Eishinkai in December and beginning Iaido training. From 1978, he received instruction from Matsuo Haruna Sensei in Okayama Prefecture. In 1988, he joined the Buko Dosoki (Martial Alumni Association). He met Secretary-General Tadahiko Morita Sensei, the head of the dojo, and subsequently received instruction from Chief Instructor Tomiyoshi Tomigahara Sensei.

In 1989, he participated in a seminar in Dartford, England, following Sensei Haruna.

He passed his 8th Dan in May 2008. He then won the 19th Hakone 8th Dan Tournament in May 2011.

He has participated in over 40 seminars in Europe and the Americas.

Oshita Sensei has supported students in numerous countries outside of Japan and often looks after them when they come to Japan.
