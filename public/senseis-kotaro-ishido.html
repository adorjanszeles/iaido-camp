Kotaro Ishido Sensei

Name
Kotaro Ishido
Born
January 1974, Kanagawa Prefecture
Iaido
Kyoshi 7th Dan
Jodo
Kyoshi 7th Dan

In 1978, Kotaro Sensei began practicing Kendo at the Shinbukan Ishido Dojo at the age of four.

He received instruction from his grandfather, Sadataro Sensei, and his father, Shizufumi Sensei.

In 1984, at the age of ten, he began basic training in Iaido and Jodo.

In 2006, he became the Fuku Kancho (deputy director) of the Shinbukan Ishido Dojo. He began accompanying Kancho Shizufumi Sensei to European seminars.

He continued to receive instruction from Shizufumi Sensei, and in 2020 he obtained 7th Dan in both Iaido and Jodo.

He is dedicated to his Iaido and Jodo training.
