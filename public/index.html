Invitation - Iaido & Jodo Summer Seminar 2026

In the summer of 2026 we will have the honor to welcome Shizufumi Ishido Sensei and members of the Japanese delegation to Budapest for a special Iaido and Jodo seminar.

The seminar will take place at the Ludovika Arena, where practitioners from many countries will gather to train together in a friendly and respectful atmosphere.

This event is organized with the intention of creating an opportunity to practice sincerely, deepen understanding, and share the spirit of budo through direct training with senior teachers.

We warmly invite all practitioners of Iaido and Jodo to join us.

A Friendly Gathering of Practitioners

We hope this seminar will provide an opportunity for practitioners from different countries to meet, train together, and strengthen the international Iaido and Jodo community.

Everyone is welcome to participate in the spirit of mutual respect and learning.

We look forward to practicing together in Budapest.
