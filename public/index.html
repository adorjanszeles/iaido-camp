Welcome to the Ishido Sensei - Summer Seminar 2026

0 days 0 hours 0 minutes 0 seconds

Join us for an unforgettable Iaido/Jodo experience in the summer of 2026. Budapest and the magnificent Ludovika Arena will host one of Europe's most significant martial arts events of the year: the summer seminar led by Ishido Shizufumi Sensei (Iaido Hanshi 8th Dan, Jodo Hanshi 8th Dan).

This is more than a regular training camp. It is a rare opportunity to learn directly from one of the most influential figures of modern Iaido and Jodo. The seminar focuses not only on technical precision, but also on the spirit behind each movement and the transmission of pure form.

Why register?

  • World-class instruction: Learn directly from Ishido Sensei and the distinguished Japanese delegation:

Oshita Masakazu
Morishima Kazuki

  • Dual focus: Dedicated days for both Iaido and Jodo.

  • International community: Meet practitioners from across Europe.
