Yuko Inari began her Iaido journey unexpectedly. Seeking to escape Kendo and her grandfather's watchful eye by moving to Kyoto for Ryukoku University, she was lured by a free meal, only to be ambushed by Morita Sensei.

Barking, "If you aren't doing Kendo, you must do Iaido!", he sealed her fate.

She soon found herself training at the Rokushikan Dojo, where she met lifelong friends like Takizawa-san.

Her overseas seminar debut was in Brighton in 2005. Thanks to an introduction from Morita Sensei, she attended the Brighton seminar alongside Oshita Sensei, Bryer-san, and Onishi-san, where she met Ishido Sensei for the very first time.

During this trip, she famously overslept and, mistakenly thinking she had been left behind, happily enjoyed a beer at the university bar. Meanwhile, Morita Sensei had been worried sick about her, earning her a resounding "Bakayaro!" when he finally found her.

Now based in Kawasaki and training at the Ishido Dojo, Yuko has accompanied Ishido Sensei to over ten overseas seminars.

Having reached 7th Dan despite the constant scolding, she deeply treasures the bonds formed with friends and teachers worldwide.
