Sayonara Party - Participant Information & Menu

The official Saturday night social event and joint dinner of the Summer Seminar 2026 will be held at the premium Trofea Grill Event House Zuglo.

Throughout the evening, an all-you-can-eat and drink experience awaits all practitioners and members of the Japanese delegation, complete with a live show grill and a fantastic community atmosphere.

Venue and Location

  • Name: Trofea Grill Event House Zuglo
  • Address: Budapest, Erzsebet kiralyne utja 5, 1145, Hungary

Arrival and Welcome

Upon arrival at the venue, the Event House staff will welcome the seminar participants with special refreshments:

  • Welcome Drink: A choice of sparkling wine, soft drink, or mineral water served upon arrival.
  • Welcome Savories: Freshly baked Hungarian cheese scones (pogacsa).

Buffet Selection (All You Can Eat)

The entire buffet selection is available for unlimited consumption. The extensive menu features traditional Hungarian specialties, international dishes, and vegetarian delicacies. The diversity of the buffet also ensures that guests with food sensitivities and dietary restrictions can easily find suitable options.

Appetizers, Soups, and Salads

  • Cold Appetizers: Caprese mozzarella, chicken and bean terrine, traditional Hungarian cold platter (cracklings, salami, sausage, bacon, and sliced ham), goose liver pate with cranberries, goose liver terrine, cheese wrap, steak tartare, smoked salmon, ham rolls with stuffed eggs, and a selection of two spreads/dips.
  • Soups: Clear beef soup, traditional Hungarian goulash soup, turkey ragout soup with tarragon, and chilled fruit soup.
  • Salads and Pickles: Greek salad, Caesar salad, potato salad with mayonnaise, corn salad with mayonnaise, coleslaw, tzatziki, fermented cucumber pickles, pickled apple peppers, and beetroot salad.
  • Fresh Vegetable Selection: Red onion, cucumber, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, rocket (arugula), and baby beet leaves.

Sushi and Seafood Selection

  • Sushi: 8-10 varieties of fresh sushi, including maki, nigiri, and inside-out rolls.
  • Seafood Specialities: Surimi crab salad, sesame seaweed salad, tapioca pudding, and salmon with spinach and ricotta.

Hot Main Courses and Side Dishes

  • Main Courses: Burgundy-style venison roast with potato fritters, venison stew with egg barley (tarhonya), roast duck with braised cabbage, Hortobagy-style savory pancakes filled with meat, pork neck slices with mustard sauce, chicken breast stuffed with ham and cheese served with mushroom and spinach sauce, mac and cheese, and chicken leg in hunter-style sauce with bread dumplings.
  • Side Dishes: Steamed rice, mashed potatoes, braised red cabbage, steamed vegetables, steamed baby carrots, steamed sweet corn, and baked French-style spinach gratin.

Live Show Grill

Sizzling grill specialties prepared freshly on the spot by the chefs:

  • Pepper-crusted beef tenderloin, garlic shrimp, grilled salmon fillet, green-lipped mussels with lemon pepper, and a seafood selection (frutti di mare).
  • Gyros-style chicken breast fillet, turkey breast fillet with sour cream marinade, pork neck steak with Dijon mustard, grilled potatoes, grilled vegetables, and grilled cheese.

Desserts and Fruits

  • Sweet Delicacies: Somloi sponge cake trifle, profiteroles, traditional Hungarian vanilla slice (kremes), tiramisu, lemon squares, Belgian chocolate specialties, forest fruit cake, Swiss roll sponge cake, and doughnuts filled with hazelnut cream.
  • Allergen-Friendly Options: 4-5 varieties of allergen-friendly and special dietary desserts.
  • Fresh Seasonal Fruits: Strawberries, watermelon, cantaloupe melon, pineapple, oranges, grapefruit, kiwi, apples, and pears.

Price and Packages

  • Base Price: 69 EUR / person (fully includes the entire buffet menu, the standard all-you-can-drink package, and the service fee).
  • Optional Palinka Spirits Coupon Package: To enhance your experience, you can purchase a 10-piece palinka spirits coupon package for 30 EUR. Each package includes 10 coupons redeemable for palinka spirits/shots at the bar. Depending on your preference, multiple 10-piece coupon packages can be purchased during registration or at the venue.

Important Policy

Please note that bringing outside alcoholic beverages or spirits into the venue is strictly prohibited.
