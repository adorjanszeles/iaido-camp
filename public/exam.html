Information on Iaido and Jodo Grading Examinations

Hungarian Kendo, Iaido and Jodo Federation (HKF)

Dates: Jodo: August 1st, 2026 | Iaido: August 3rd, 2026

1. Examination Fees (for non-HKF members)

The grading fees consist of the Application Fee and the Registration Fee, both payable only upon registration.

Grade Application Fee Registration Fee Total Fee
1. Dan 15 EUR 10 EUR 25 EUR
2. Dan 25 EUR 15 EUR 40 EUR
3. Dan 40 EUR 20 EUR 60 EUR
4. Dan 65 EUR 35 EUR 100 EUR
5. Dan 90 EUR 75 EUR 165 EUR

Note: The application fee is non-refundable regardless of the result.

2. Requirements for Application

Membership: Candidates must be registered members of an EKF/FIK affiliated national federation with no outstanding fees.

Waiting Period: Minimum time since the last successful examination must be fulfilled according to FIK/EKF rules.

Official Registration: All official applications must be submitted by National Federations through the standard EKF admin system no later than 2 weeks before the event. Approval from the National Federation is managed directly through this system. EKF admin system.

3. Payment

Only cash payment which can be paid to the organizers during the seminar.

4. Examination Content

The technical examinations follow Zen Nippon Kendo Renmei (ZNKR) and EKF grading rules and standards: EKF grading rules.

Iaido

1st - 5th Dan: 5 ZNKR Seitei Iaido forms, selected by the head of jury.

No applied time limit.

Note regarding 4th and 5th Dan: The inclusion of Koryu forms is subject to the specific decision of the examination committee.

Jodo

1st - 5th Dan: 5 ZNKR Jodo forms (shitei waza).

Candidates must perform the forms in both roles (Shi and Uchi).
